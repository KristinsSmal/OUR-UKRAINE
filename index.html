Our tours

Askania-Nova Biosphere Reserve

This reserve on the territory of Ukraine is unique. For every visitor Askania-Nova offers the opportunity to see not only the arboretum and zoo, but also the virgin steppe. He retained his original inviolability. Here you can see the animals in their natural guise.

Where: Kherson region, Chaplinsky district, Askania-Nova township. Book now
Synevyr National Nature Park

Synevyr is a park of incredible beauty. There is everything: mountains, forest, lake and swamps. Once upon a time, this park was a seabed, and now everyone can walk through it, breathe fresh air and reunite with nature. The only "Forest and Rafting Museum" in Europe is located in the park.. Where: Transcarpathian region, Mizhhirya district. Book now

Shatsky National Nature Park

Shatsky National Nature Park is exactly the place where you can relax not only body but also soul. Incredible landscapes will amaze even those people who do not love nature. The eco-system of lake-swamp-forest complexes in this park is considered to be the largest in Europe.

Where: in the Volyn region. Book now
Carpathian National Nature Park

The Carpathian National Park is not only an incredible nature with lakes, unique cedar pines, waterfalls and rocks. It is a concentration of ancient archeological monuments, as well as monuments of wooden folk architecture of the XVI-XIX centuries.

Where: Ivano-Frankivsk region, Yaremche, Hutsul region. Book now
